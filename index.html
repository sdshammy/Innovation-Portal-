AI-Powered Document Processing System

Sarah Johnson

Legal Department

Description

Implement machine learning to automate contract review and extraction of key terms, reducing legal team workload by 40%. The system would:

  • Analyze incoming contracts and highlight key clauses
  • Extract important dates, parties, and obligations automatically
  • Flag non-standard terms for human review
  • Integrate with our existing document management system

Estimated development time: 3-4 months. Potential ROI within 6 months of implementation.
